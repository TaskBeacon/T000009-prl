Title: Probabilistic Reversal Learning (PRL) Task
Construct: reinforcement learning / cognitive flexibility

Purpose:
Show the participant-visible PRL trial timeline with AB/BA side mapping and probabilistic feedback.

Rows / conditions:
- AB: stimulus A on the left, stimulus B on the right.
- BA: stimulus B on the left, stimulus A on the right.

Timeline:
- Fixation: random 600-800 ms; no response; centered +.
- Choice: 1500 ms; press F for left or J for right; two symbols shown side by side; selected side is highlighted.
- Blank: random 400-600 ms; no response; blank screen.
- Feedback: 800 ms; no response; +10, -10, or no response -10.

Notes:
- Higher-probability option wins with 80% probability before reversal and 90% after reversal.
- Reversal occurs after 9 correct choices within the last 10 trials.
- No response produces -10 feedback.
